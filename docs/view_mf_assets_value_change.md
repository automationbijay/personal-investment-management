# Mutual Fund Asset Value Change (`view_mf_assets_value_change`)

The `view_mf_assets_value_change` is a foundational PostgreSQL View that tracks the precise performance of every individual stock holding inside a mutual fund's equity portfolio.

It bridges the gap between the static monthly portfolio disclosures and real-time market data by calculating the exact monetary gain or loss for each stock since the mutual fund last published its Weekly NAV. 

This view serves as the base data layer that feeds into the aggregated `view_mf_summary_analytics` view.

---

## Column Breakdown & Calculation Logic

### 1. Asset Identifiers & Holdings
* **`MF`**: The ticker symbol of the Mutual Fund that holds the asset (e.g., `NICSF`, `C30MF`). Sourced from NEPSEAlpha portfolio disclosures (`raw_mf_nepsealpha_assets_lastmonth`).
* **`symbol`**: The ticker symbol of the underlying stock/scrip held by the mutual fund (e.g., `SHPC`, `NABIL`).
* **`quantity`**: The exact number of shares of the stock held by the mutual fund, as per their last monthly disclosure.

### 2. Historical Context (Weekly NAV Baseline)
* **`Monthly_Nav_Date`**: A descriptive string representing the Nepali month (e.g., "Jestha 2083") of the last official portfolio disclosure.
* **`Weekly_Nav`**: The official Weekly NAV figure published on ShareSansar.
* **`Weekly_Nav_Date`**: The specific date associated with the published Weekly NAV (Format: `MM/DD/YYYY`).
* **`price_on_weekly_nav`**: The historical end-of-day closing price of the underlying stock (`symbol`) on the exact `Weekly_Nav_Date`. This is pulled dynamically from the `raw_price_history` table.
* **`value_on_weekly_nav`**: The baseline monetary value of the holding at the time the Weekly NAV was calculated.
  * *Formula*: `quantity * price_on_weekly_nav`

### 3. Real-Time Valuation (Today's Performance)
* **`todays_ltp`**: The current Live Traded Price (LTP) of the underlying stock on the secondary market. This updates continuously during market hours via the `raw_nepseapi_live_prices` table.
* **`value_today`**: The real-time monetary value of the holding based on current market conditions.
  * *Formula*: `quantity * todays_ltp`

### 4. Granular Performance Metrics
* **`value_change_since_weekly`**: The absolute monetary gain or loss generated by this specific holding since the mutual fund published its last Weekly NAV.
  * *Formula*: `value_today - value_on_weekly_nav`
  * *Purpose*: By summing this column across all assets held by a specific `MF`, we determine exactly how much the equity portion of the mutual fund's portfolio has grown or shrunk in NPR value since the last official valuation.

---

## Data Dependencies
The view dynamically joins the following tables to generate its metrics in real-time:
1. `raw_mf_nepsealpha_assets_lastmonth` (Portfolio composition & quantities)
2. `raw_mf_sharesansar_nav` (Published NAVs & Dates)
3. `raw_price_history` (Historical End-of-Day prices for the baseline calculation)
4. `raw_nepseapi_live_prices` (Real-time prices for the current valuation)
